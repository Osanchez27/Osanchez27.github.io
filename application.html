FAQ

Sound too good to be true?

How do I share my files?

Simple.
Either drag & drop them into our uploader, or click the upload box to select your file. Once the upload is complete, we will issue you a unique URL which you can share with others who can then download the file instantly.

How long will my files be online?

Up to 30 days for free users..
Free users can upload files and choose that they auto expire after 1 hour, 1 day, 7 days, or 30 days.

What can I upload?

Anything.
Just keep it legal. Anonymously.io was built with love, to allow everybody to be able to share their files.

We'll be able to keep offering the service as long as the files shared aren't breaking any laws.

Can I email my files?

Yes.
You can of course email your file URLs, however we do not offer an email service directly (yet). Instead you can use our "Add this" plugin or you can simply copy the URL into your email client.

Is Anonymously.io really free?

Yes.
The core service is completely free,
there are no hidden costs to host your files.

How do I download a file?

Click download.
It's very easy (yet we still get asked this a lot). When you've been sent a file URL, simply click the "download" button.

Why was my file removed?

Complaints.
Files must adhere to our terms of service in order to remain hosted on Anonymously.io, repeatedly reported files will be automatically removed.

How many downloads per file?

Unlimited.
We do not impose bandwidth limits, your uploads can be continuously downloaded until the file is either deleted, or it expires.

Are there log files or backups?

No.
All uploads are completely anonymous, our logs are cycled continuously. Once a file has automatically been deleted, it is completely gone, forever.
